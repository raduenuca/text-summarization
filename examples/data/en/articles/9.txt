Allianz-Tiriac Asigurari has been the President of UNSAR since 1997, the only exception being a short period of time during which ING Asigurari de Viata held this position.The General Assembly also voted the new members of the Board of Directors. Thus, the newly elected Board of Directors includes Allianz-Tiriac Asigurari, ING Asigurari de Viata, BCR Asigurari de Viata, Generali Romania, Gothaer Asigurari - Reasigurari, Grawe Romania and Uniqa Asigurari. The Board of Directors carries out the operational activity between two General Assemblies, being responsible for the implementation of the General Assembly's decisions.During the same meeting, Eurolife ERB Asigurari de Viata was accepted as a new Associated Member of UNSAR, joining Credit Europe Asigurari and Asito Kapital and thus increasing the associated members' number to three.The National Association of Insurance and Reinsurance Companies in Romania is a professional association founded in 1994. It currently represents the interest of 24 profile companies which have, together, a share of over 90% of the Romanian insurance market.
