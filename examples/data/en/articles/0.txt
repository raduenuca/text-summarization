CHISINAU (Moldova), June 7 (SeeNews) – The Moldovan Securities Commission, CNPF, said it has endorsed the acquisition of a 99.93% stake in Romania's Asito Kapital by Moldovan insurer Moldasig.

The acquisition of 97,053 Asito Kapital shares is also pending approval by the Romanian insurance regulator, CSA.

Moldasig is 80%-owned by Russian insurer Rosgosstrakh. Moldasig was the country’s largest insurer in the first quarter this year in terms of premium income.

UK-based Norcross Insurance currently holds 99.93% in Asito Kapital.
